OUR STORY

It was over a half century ago that Sri Lanka's foremost baker and caterer embarked on a journey to serve Sri lanka with the best possible meals. Founded on traditions and values that are still upheld by all those who fall under the Nipun Rajapakshe umbrella,we make sure every item of food we produce has that extra bit of personal commitment and care.

Generations of Sri lanka have grown up with our flavours and we have always valued our customer relationships and pay tribute to all those who have believed in us. To this end we strive to be a source of delectable meals and great memories with friends and family.
